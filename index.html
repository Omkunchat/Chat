Technical Deep Dive & FAQs

Everything you need to know about the Omkun Chat engine.

Are there any hidden markups on WhatsApp message costs?
No. Unlike other platforms that add a 15-20% margin, Omkun Chat connects directly to your Meta Business account. You pay Meta’s official conversation rates directly through your own credit card, ensuring 100% pricing transparency.
How does the Round Robin assignment logic work?
Our system uses an automated circular distribution algorithm. When a customer requires human assistance, the chat is assigned to the next available agent in your team of 10, ensuring an even workload and faster response times.
Can the AI handle payments and order confirmations?
Yes. Omkun Chat integrates natively with Razorpay and Stripe. The AI can calculate total amounts, generate a payment button within the chat, and automatically update the order status in your Kanban CRM once the payment is successful.
Is my business data secure on your platform?
Security is our top priority. We utilize Cloudflare Workers for edge computing and Firebase for secure database management. All WhatsApp communications are routed through official Meta Cloud API channels with end-to-end encryption protocols.
