Official Meta Rules

WhatsApp Business API Guidelines

Stay compliant and avoid account bans. Follow these official Meta rules to ensure a smooth, uninterrupted messaging experience.

The 24-Hour Session Window

When a customer sends a message to your WhatsApp number, a 24-hour customer service window opens. Inside this window:

  • You can send free-form messages (text, media, files) without prior template approval.
  • The 24-hour timer resets every time the customer replies.
Important: Outside this 24-hour window, you can ONLY send pre-approved "Template Messages" (like order updates or marketing broadcasts).

User Opt-In Requirements

You must receive active consent (opt-in) from users before sending them proactive outbound templates.

Allowed Opt-ins

Checkbox on website checkout, SMS opt-in, IVR selection, or customers clicking a "Chat on WhatsApp" button.

Not Allowed

Buying cold databases, scraping numbers from the internet, or assuming consent from old business cards.

Prohibited Categories

Meta strictly prohibits businesses dealing in the following categories from using the WhatsApp Business API. If your business falls into these, your account will be rejected:

Drugs & Tobacco Gambling Weapons & Ammunition Adult Content Medical/Healthcare (Restrictions apply)
